## Writing Procedure
The writing procedure is quite different from what I have done before. Similar to most writers, I make the mistake that I want write a perfect paper at the first place. This is not an ideal procedure to write. The mistake by most writers is that they want to make their paper perfect at the first place. This means you work at many aspects when you are writing. You care about many following aspects. This is not recommended because writing is to organize and present your ideas from your subconcious. If you focus too much on the readability, you lost your clue and the article becomes irrational, lack of natural evolvement, and lack of logical flows. So the most important thing to start writing is to write without break. Just write what came to your mind and do not stop. This is an effective way to solve writer's block, which is the most negative feelings that most writers want to avoid. Also in practice, this proves a great success. Many of my great sentences are written in this style. When I am writing without concerning too much stuff in mind, the result is actually even better than what I tried hard to think, and look up at others. The resulting sentences are short, concise, and complete. It conveys exactly the ideas that I want to express. I can think of a lot more examples in my thesis. It is an entertainment to read these sentences. So the first principle is to do not be lazy. Write a 1/2 draft and revise it later. There are also another 1 draft and 1 review step from the TUM lecture. I did not learn it well. Maybe I will revisit it later.

## Cohesion, Sentence, and Credibility
